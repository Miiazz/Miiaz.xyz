About Me

Hey, I’m Michael an Engineering Technology student with hands-on experience in mechanical design, embedded systems, and process improvement.

I combine a decade’s worth of marine industry experience with a technical background in CAD, Python, and Linux, bringing a practical perspective to real-world engineering challenges and processes.

This website documents my personal projects, each designed to reflect the breadth of Engineering Technology by tackling a wide range of problems using an equally wide range of tools.

I’m currently pursuing a Certificate in Cybersecurity & Cyberforensics, expanding into digital forensics, network defense, and system hardening — with the goal of becoming more versatile in modern work environments and building smarter, more efficient systems.

Looking ahead, I’m focused on completing my EIT certification and continuing to develop cross-disciplinary projects that bridge mechanical, electrical, and digital systems — with the goal of turning creative concepts into functional, real-world solutions.

Focus Areas

  • Mechanical Design: CAD modeling, 3D printing, rapid prototyping
  • Embedded Systems: Raspberry Pi / ESP32 / microcontrollers
  • Cybersecurity: Network analysis, digital forensics
  • Software Tools: Python, C++, Bash, Linux
