AR500 Steel Plate — Reverse-Engineered Drawing

A small freelance project where I was tasked with reverse-engineering an existing AR500 plate and creating a dimensioned drawing suitable for fabrication. The plate was measured using calipers and a tape measure, and intended dimensions were inferred based on standard practices for similar plates as well as manufacturerability considerations. The final deliverable included practical tolerancing, clearly defined & labeled dimensions, and production friendly notes.

  • Type: Reverse engineering & drafting
  • Material: AR500, Abrasion Resistant Steel 500 BHN
  • Thickness: 3/8" (0.375")
  • Deliverable: Dimensioned drawing (JPEG / PDF-ready)
  • Focus: Practical tolerancing, clear bolt pattern layout, shop-friendly notes
